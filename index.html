I always find it a bit difficult to succinctly express my interests, since they are broad and volatile. My previous research experiences include some spent at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, where I worked under Dr. Simon C. Jones developing a novel electrochemical method for converting CO2 to O2 on Mars. Additionally, I had a roughly two year affiliation with the Born Lab at Harvard Medical School working for Dr. Richard T. Born on a trial-by-trial analysis of a visual decision-making task using dynamic logistic regression.
